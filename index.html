What is GPT-3

GPT-3 (Generative Pre-trained Transformer 3) is a powerful language model by OpenAI. It generates human-like text based on input, enabling applications like chatbots, content creation, and translation. With 175 billion parameters, it's one of the largest and most advanced AI models for natural language processing.
